\chapter{Abstract}

This thesis aims to analyze the evolution of video game industry revenues over the period 2014–2024, with the objective of identifying the main factors driving this growth. Particular attention is given to macroeconomic, COVID-19-related, and socio-demographic variables.
\\\\
To achieve this, a quantitative analysis is conducted using regression models, incorporating indicators such as the Stringency Index, GNI per capita, Gini Index, depression rates, and population size.
\\\\
The results suggest that the COVID-19 pandemic had a significant positive impact on industry revenues, primarily driven by increased time spent at home and changes in consumption patterns.\\
However, this positive impact appears to diminish as the Stringency Index decreases and pandemic-related restrictions are lifted.
\\\\
Overall, this study contributes to the existing literature by providing a broader macroeconomic perspective on the determinants of growth in the video game industry before, during and after the pandemic.
